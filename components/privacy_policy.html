Dubai Customs Wallet Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Dubai Customs Wallet Extension handles your data. We collect only the information necessary to provide our services.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal data when you access or use the Dubai Customs Wallet services. By using our services, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Dubai Customs Wallet:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details provided during registration.
  • Transaction Data: Information about any transactions performed, including amounts and payment methods.
  • Device Information: Device details such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.

What We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Full Name
  • ❌ Email Address
  • ❌ Location Data (unless explicitly enabled by the user)

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To manage and enhance your Dubai Customs Wallet account.
  • To process transactions and provide customer support.
  • To improve and secure our services, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • To send updates, notifications, or promotional offers, if opted in.

How We Protect Your Information

We employ robust security measures to safeguard your information:

  • Data encryption to protect sensitive information.
  • Secure servers and advanced firewall technology to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits to ensure the integrity of our systems.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties except in the following situations:

  • When required by law or legal process (e.g., a subpoena or court order).
  • With trusted service providers who help operate our platform and provide services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The extension does not use tracking technologies beyond what is necessary for functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated version will include a "Last Revised" date.
